Catalog of Shoes

In 1970, the adidas Superstar shoes made their debut on the hardwood court for basketball. They quickly transitioned from athletic clothing to a mainstay of streetwear. These shoes exhibit the components, proportions, and design that helped establish the original as a legend. Smooth leather covers the uppers, which also include a heel tab and athletic 3-Stripes. They are completed with the renowned rubber shell toe.

Of the most recognizable patterns in a store full of them. The Old Skool evolved from the Vans #36, which debuted the now-iconic Vans Sidestripe in 1977. Vans Old Skool Black & White is more evidence that the Classics never go out of style. The Vans Old Skool Black & White sneaker has endured the test of time thanks to the union of sturdy suede and canvas, and founder Paul Van Doren's efforts guarantee its continued popularity. With reinforced toe caps, supportive cushioned colorways, and recognizable rubber waffle outsoles for optimal comfort and convenience, Vans Old Skool Black & White represents Vans at its best.

The Nike Streakfly, the lightest racing shoe, is all about the speed you need to beat the opposition in a mile, 5k, or 10k event. To help you better connect with the road as you work toward your personal best, this shoe's low profile and elegant accents provide the impression that it vanishes on your foot.

These platform Chucks are sure to draw attention because they were inspired by the classic styles of '90s girl bands. A double-stacked sole lifts you higher, and canvas can be as elegant or edgy as you choose.

The iconic look of Michael Jordan's first signature shoe is combined with a simple, one-handed entry technique in the Air Jordan 1 Hi FlyEase. Just a wraparound zipper and two straps are used to create a specific fit; there are no laces to tie.

Gym culture-related. Perfect for deadlifts. These adidas The Total weightlifting shoes help produce the ideal low-to-the-ground stance desired by many deadlifters thanks to their zero heel raise. You can also find a steady posture with the wide toe box's assistance. The sturdy textile upper, which features gym-inspired design elements, uses a strap and laces to keep you securely fastened in.
